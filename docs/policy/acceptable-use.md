# Acceptable Use Policy — CuePoint

**Version 1.0 — 2026-02-03**  
**Last updated**: 2026-02-03

## Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use of CuePoint and its associated services.

## Acceptable Use

You may use CuePoint to:

- Enrich your Rekordbox playlists with Beatport metadata
- Process your own music library data locally
- Run the application for personal or commercial use within the scope of its intended functionality

## Prohibited Use

You must not:

1. **Abuse External Services**: Do not use CuePoint to make excessive requests to Beatport, DuckDuckGo, or other third-party services that could violate their terms or cause harm.
2. **Automate at Scale**: Do not run automated bulk processing or scraping that could overload or degrade third-party services.
3. **Circumvent Restrictions**: Do not use CuePoint to bypass rate limits, access controls, or terms of service of external services.
4. **Malicious Use**: Do not use CuePoint to distribute malware, violate privacy, or engage in illegal activities.

## Compliance

If you use CuePoint in a way that violates this policy or the terms of third-party services, we may discontinue support or access. We reserve the right to update this policy as needed.

## Contact

For questions about acceptable use, please open an issue in the CuePoint repository.
