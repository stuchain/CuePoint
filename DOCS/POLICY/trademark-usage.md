# Trademark and Brand Usage — CuePoint

**Version 1.0 — 2026-02-03**  
**Last updated**: 2026-02-03

## Overview

This document provides guidance on using the CuePoint name, logo, and brand assets.

## Permitted Use

You may use the CuePoint name and logo when:

- **Referring to the software**: Describing or linking to the CuePoint application.
- **Including in documentation**: Tutorials, guides, or documentation that reference CuePoint.
- **Contributing**: As part of contributions to the CuePoint project.

## Restrictions

- **Do not imply endorsement**: Do not use the CuePoint name or logo in a way that suggests official endorsement unless you have permission.
- **Do not modify**: Do not alter the logo (colors, proportions, elements) without permission.
- **Do not use for commercial branding**: Do not use CuePoint branding for unrelated products or services in a way that could cause confusion.

## Brand Assets

- **Logo**: Located in `DOCS/images/logo.png`
- **Usage**: Use the official logo when linking to or referencing CuePoint.

## Contact

For questions about trademark or brand usage, open an issue in the CuePoint repository.
